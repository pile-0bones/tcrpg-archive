Pan

The Shadowfell is known for the horror that plagues its lands. Countless creatures of terrifying might and unholy power reign from its depths, waiting for the opportunity to strike against those who wander too close. It's hard to imagine a creature of goodness to emerge from this dark plane--and yet, a true hero of Eyzeldon had indeed come from the darkness of the Shadowfell. With her superhuman skills as a marksman, arcano-technological prosthetic arm, and her trusty Griffin steed Archie, Pan is not only a top-notch messenger of the Amber Flame, but a true force to be reckoned with.
