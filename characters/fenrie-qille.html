Fenrie Q'ille

This is where the description of the character goes. It should start out with an epic story that encapsulates the kind of fighter the character is. A few sentences of that, hyping up their strengths such as their powerful spell casting, impressive feats of strength, stealth ability, or other things of that note. It should be a few sentences. Then, we're introducted to them: Their name, their goal, their "thing". Mention their involvement with their party, and whether or not their story has been completed. It should make the player of this character feel good about what they've created. We love this guy, and so should you! Then end it off once you feel like you've made a good show
