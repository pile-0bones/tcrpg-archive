Sybil Ascendant

A group of hooded cultists stand hunched over a bloodstone pedestal. On it, the subject of their ritual: hoardes of corpses, ready to be raised into beastly undead to do their masters' dastardly bidding. They chant louder and louder, the gnarled staffs in their hands glowing with unholy light. Just as their chant nearly approaches its climax, BAM! In an instant, a smoking hole appears between a cultist's eyes. Without any time for the dark mages to react, the shadow of two enormous ivory wings loom above them. No prayer to their terrible deity could save them now. This is Sybil Ascendant: Sharpshooter, Adventurer, and Chosen of the Raven Queen.
