Here you'll find every supplementary creation I've homebrewed for my campaigns. There's plenty of stuff that has been lost to time (always back up your files, folks!) but this will eventually have everything I can find. Feel free to use any of it in your own games--I'd love to hear about it if you do! Feedback on how to better balance anything in here is welcome. I'm sure some of the earlier stuff I've created is nigh-unusable.
